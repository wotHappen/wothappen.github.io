What is WotHappen?

Well, the question should be: Who is WotHappen?

It's a bot integrated in the Cisco Spark platform that compresses conversations to give you a concise summary of the entire chat history. Powered by NLP and big data analysis, it's like an incredibly detailed set of notes on a lengthy classroom lecture, concisely describing the occurrences inside a timeframe and providing a systematic overview of an otherwise uninspiring stream of text.

In addition, it analyzes the ongoing sentiment and mood of a conversation by leveraging Microsoft's Cognitive Services API to identify emotions in the messages extracted from the chat history, and conveys them in a easily readable way (with emojis!). So now, you know which chatrooms are the hubs of heated debate, or wholesome appreciation, or plain, calm discussion.

WotHappen was built as a project at CalHacks 4.0.

Features

Check out what goes into our effort to make chatting great again

Captures the Point

WotHappen uses NLP to provide an extraction-based summary and abstraction of your chat history, in just 5 sentences.

Identifies Emotions

WotHappen can gather the thousands of messages in your chat and provide a sentimental analysis of how the overall mood of the conversation is.

Always Ready

WotHappen is integrated in the Cisco Spark platform, so you can instantly use the bot in the app. Just add it to your chat, and you can immediately start using it to gather insights!

Understands Your Needs

WotHappen is designed to not just be an interface to your chat, but also a conversational assistant. So, feel free to address it with your most natural requests, like "Show me the summary of this conversation". Alternatively, you can also use simple command-based messages to talk to the bot.
